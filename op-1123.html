Why the Anime is on Hiatus

The One Piece anime has been on hiatus since October 2024 and will resume in April 2025. The break allows the production team to enhance the quality of upcoming episodes and prevent the anime from catching up too closely to the manga.

During this hiatus, fans can enjoy a **remastered** and **re-edited** version of the **Fish-Man Island** arc, which has been condensed into **21 episodes** with improved visuals and audio.
